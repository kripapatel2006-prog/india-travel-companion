🏔️ Manali

Experience snow-capped mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports and peaceful Himalayan landscapes.

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to June

💰 Average Budget

₹8,000 – ₹20,000 for a 4-day trip.

🍜 Must Try Food

Siddu, Trout Fish, Thukpa, Momos and Babru.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Solang Valley
  • Rohtang Pass
  • Hadimba Temple
  • Old Manali
  • Jogini Waterfall

🚕 Local Transport

Taxis, rental bikes, local buses and walking are the best ways to explore Manali.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes throughout the year.
  • Book hotels in advance during peak season.
  • Check weather before visiting Rohtang Pass.
  • Carry cash for local markets.

🍽️ Famous Food Places to Try

📍 Johnson's Café

⭐ Famous for Trout Fish and Continental cuisine.

📍 Café 1947

⭐ Popular riverside café with Italian food and coffee.

📍 Chopsticks Restaurant

⭐ Famous for Tibetan food, Momos and Thukpa.

📍 Mount View Restaurant

⭐ Great place for authentic Himachali food.
