🏖️ Goa

India's most loved beach destination with beautiful sunsets, nightlife and adventure.

📅 Best Time to Visit

November to February

💰 Average Budget

₹6,000 – ₹15,000 for a 3-day trip.

🍛 Must Try Food

Goan Fish Curry, Bebinca, Prawn Balchão, Vindaloo.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Baga Beach
  • Calangute Beach
  • Dudhsagar Falls
  • Fort Aguada
  • Anjuna Beach

🚕 Local Transport

Scooters, taxis, buses and rental bikes are easily available.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Carry sunscreen.
  • Book hotels early during December.
  • Respect beach cleanliness.
  • Carry cash for local markets.
