🏔️ Ladakh

Experience breathtaking mountains, crystal-clear lakes, ancient monasteries and unforgettable road trips.

📅 Best Time to Visit

May to September

💰 Average Budget

₹12,000 – ₹30,000 for a 4-day trip.

🍜 Must Try Food

Momos, Thukpa, Butter Tea, Skyu.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Pangong Lake
  • Nubra Valley
  • Khardung La Pass
  • Magnetic Hill
  • Shanti Stupa

🚕 Local Transport

Taxis, rented bikes, shared cabs and local buses are the best ways to travel.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Spend the first day acclimatizing.
  • Carry warm clothes even in summer.
  • Keep a valid ID proof.
  • Stay hydrated because of the high altitude.

🍽️ Famous Food Places to Try

📍 The Tibetan Kitchen

⭐ Famous for Momos, Thukpa and authentic Tibetan cuisine.

📍 Gesmo Restaurant

⭐ Popular for Tibetan, Indian and Continental dishes.

📍 Bon Appetit

⭐ Scenic mountain-view restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine.

📍 Lamayuru Restaurant

⭐ Great place to enjoy authentic Ladakhi food and Butter Tea.
