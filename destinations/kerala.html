🌴 Kerala

God's Own Country famous for backwaters, tea plantations, beaches and rich culture.

📅 Best Time to Visit

September to March

💰 Average Budget

₹7,000 – ₹18,000 for a 3-day trip.

🍛 Must Try Food

Appam, Puttu, Kerala Sadya, Fish Curry.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Munnar
  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Kochi
  • Wayanad
  • Varkala Beach

🚕 Local Transport

KSRTC buses, ferries, taxis, auto-rickshaws and rental scooters are easily available.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Carry light cotton clothes.
  • Try a houseboat stay.
  • Carry an umbrella during monsoon.
  • Taste authentic Kerala Sadya.
