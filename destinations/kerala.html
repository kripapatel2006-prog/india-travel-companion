🌴 Kerala

God's Own Country famous for backwaters, tea plantations, beaches and rich culture.

📅 Best Time to Visit

September to March

💰 Average Budget

₹7,000 – ₹18,000 for a 3-day trip.

🍛 Must Try Food

Appam, Puttu, Kerala Sadya, Fish Curry.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Munnar
  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Kochi
  • Wayanad
  • Varkala Beach

🚕 Local Transport

KSRTC buses, ferries, taxis, auto-rickshaws and rental scooters are easily available.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Carry light cotton clothes.
  • Try a houseboat stay.
  • Carry an umbrella during monsoon.
  • Taste authentic Kerala Sadya.

🍽️ Famous Food Places to Try

📍 Paragon Restaurant, Kozhikode

⭐ Famous for Kerala Biryani and Seafood.

📍 Dhe Puttu, Kochi

⭐ Famous for Puttu varieties and Kerala cuisine.

📍 Kashi Art Café, Fort Kochi

⭐ Popular for breakfast, desserts and coffee.

📍 Grand Pavilion, Kochi

⭐ Authentic Kerala Sadya and traditional meals.
