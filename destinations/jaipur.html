🏰 Jaipur

Explore the Pink City filled with magnificent forts, royal palaces and rich Rajasthani culture.

📅 Best Time to Visit

October to March

💰 Average Budget

₹6,000 – ₹15,000 for a 3-day trip.

🍽️ Must Try Food

Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar, Pyaaz Kachori, Laal Maas.

📍 Top Attractions

  • Hawa Mahal
  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar
  • Nahargarh Fort

🚖 Local Transport

Auto-rickshaws, taxis, city buses and app-based cabs are easily available.

⚠️ Travel Tips

  • Carry sunscreen and water.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for forts.
  • Visit Amber Fort early morning.
  • Bargain while shopping in local markets.

🍽️ Famous Food Places to Try

📍 Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar (LMB)

⭐ Famous for Ghewar and authentic Rajasthani sweets.

📍 Rawat Misthan Bhandar

⭐ Home of Jaipur's famous Pyaaz Kachori.

📍 Chokhi Dhani

⭐ Traditional Rajasthani village experience with authentic cuisine.

📍 Masala Chowk

⭐ One of Jaipur's best places for local street food.
